Product Attribute Analysis & Reporting

MULTI LEVEL GROUP-BY

Easily group by multiple products attributes with our application, analyze by Motif, size and color (or any other attributes) simultaneously for a clearer insight into your data..

ADVANCED FILTERS BY MULTI ATTRIBUTES

Streamline your product management with advanced filtering. Quickly locate products by specific attributes, perfect for efficient stock replenishment or assisting customers on-the-go with precise details about any attribute combinations (colors, sizes...).

Detailed attribute analysis

Gain a clearer perspective on your sales figures through insightful attribute analysis. Our application provides a comprehensive view, allowing you to better understand and interpret your sales numbers based on various attributes.
Enhance your decision-making process with a more transparent and detailed overview of your sales performance by attributes.

How to configure

1 Download the module and place the module in apps folder.
2 Before install, by default this application will only work with sales, stock and accounting apps, but you can add others like purchase by just editing this two file by any text editor.

This was done intentionally to avoid splitting this app to multiple apps

Remove "#" to uncomment the line and app will load the necessary file to work in the desired modules

__manifest__.py

__init__.py

3 After install, Open Settings > General Settings Under Stored product attribute click on Manage stored attributes
4 Store new attribute, selected the desired attribute and click on Store & Compute.
DONE You're ready to analyse by your stored attributes (filtering & group by).

How to use it

Once you have stored your desired attributes, you can go to any module analyseand start filtering and grouping by your stored attributes.
